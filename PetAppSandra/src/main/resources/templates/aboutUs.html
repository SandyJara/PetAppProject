PETXIE

Mission

"At Petxie, we believe in creating a warm and welcoming community where pet lovers come together like family. Our mission is to connect pet owners and sitters who share the same passion for animals, offering a safe and caring space for pets and their families.".

Why Us?

In Petxie, we believe in the power of a loving and caring community. Whether you're a pet owner or a pet sitter, you're part of a group that truly understands the joy of having pets.

Our platform makes it easy to connect with others and find services like walks, visits, and house sitting, all with just a few clicks.

We know how important it is to trust the people taking care of your pets. While we're still building our community, we are focused on providing a safe and reliable space for pet lovers.

Petxie is all about sharing. As a pet owner or sitter, you can offer your services and enjoy the benefits of a caring community in return.

Vision

IN PETXIE, we aim to build a growing community of pet lovers, starting in Ireland and expanding globally. Our goal is to create a trusted network where pet owners and pet sitters can connect. In the medium term, we plan to offer services like walks, visits, and housesitting. In the future, once we have a reliable community, we hope to introduce home exchanges, where travelers who love animals can offer their services in exchange for accommodation.
